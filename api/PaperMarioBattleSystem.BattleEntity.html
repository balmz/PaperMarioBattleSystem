BattleIndex

The BattleEntity's battle index, assigned from the BattleManager. If it's less than or equal to InvalidBattleIndex, the BattleEntity is not considered in battle.

This indicates its relation to BattleEntities of the same EntityType. BattleEntities with higher battle indices are behind ones with lower battle indices.

In most cases, it also corresponds to its position from the left side of the stage and its reference index in its entity list.
