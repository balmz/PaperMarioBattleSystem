Class UIFourPiecedTex

A UIElement that holds a CroppedTexture2D, which is drawn in 4 pieces.

This is for graphics that constitute 1/4 of a whole. This class assumes the graphic is the upper-left piece and rotates the other pieces according to draw them combined (Ex. top-left of "+" sign image).

Place their pivots in the center to allow them to be rotated as one.
